How It Works Initial State: The .notification-bar is rendered off-screen with transform: translateY(-100%). When showNotification is true: The [ngClass] binding adds the “show” class, which changes the transform to translateY(0). Thanks to the CSS transition, the bar slides down smoothly into view. After 3 seconds (or your desired time), your component sets showNotification back to false, and the “show” class is removed. The bar then smoothly slides back up off-screen.
